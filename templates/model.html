Limitations

  • The model estimates performance-based value from public box-score and rate statistics only.
  • It does not fully capture role, deployment, injuries, leverage, team context, contract term, free-agent timing, or cap environment.
  • Performance Value should be interpreted as a model estimate of last season's production relative to cap hit, not a definitive player valuation.
  • The ELC toggle refreshes app rankings after filtering entry-level contracts, but it does not retrain the regression.
